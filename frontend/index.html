Extract & classify

Language, amounts (incl. Bangla digits), phone numbers and intent are parsed from free text, then a priority cascade fixes the case type.

02

Match the evidence

Candidate transactions are scored on amount, counterparty and status. A single strong match wins; a tie returns insufficient_data rather than a guess.

03

Route & reply safely

Severity, department and escalation come from declarative rules. Every reply passes a scrubber that removes credential asks and unauthorized promises.
